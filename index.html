Top Dog Breeds

Is your dog among the top canine superstars in the world? Well, according to the Fdration Cynologique Internationale (FCI) or the World Canine Organization in its 2013 figures, these pooches have made it to the A-list!

Labrador Retrievers

Good natured and all-time family companion, the Labrador Retriever is a consistent favorite all over the world. This good-natured animal has a pack mentality and is known to have originated from Canada where it was bred to catch fish.

German Shepherd

Proud, fearless and intelligent: these are some of the characteristics of this athletic dog. They are happiest when there are tasks at hand and is far from being a couch potato. No wonder they serve well in the police and military, and are great as seeing eye dogs.

Golden Retrievers

Whats not to love about this gentle creature? They adore people, water and playtime. This breed is everyones darling and is perfectly suited as emotional therapy dogs, rescuer and narcotics detector.
